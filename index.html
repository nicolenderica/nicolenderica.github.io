This is the unofficial resource for this country's incoming tourists

While in Ecuador you can experience three different types of environments. You can travel along the beautiful Pacific Ocean coast and enjoy warm beaches and delicious seafood. If you like the cold weather a little better, you can also visit the Andes mountain range in our Sierra where the "Middle of the World" is located. Last but not least, you can venture into the Amazon jungle in the Eastern part of our country in the tropical rainforest.
